About BODHI SAMBAD 2026

BODHI SAMBAD 2026 is designed to build a shared understanding of China’s rapidly evolving role in South Asia. Over the past decade, the region has witnessed the emergence of overlapping economic initiatives, growing security partnerships, and shifting geopolitical dynamics.

Many existing frameworks remain fragmented, with individual countries managing their own bilateral agendas. This conference aims to create a space where scholars, policy-makers, and civil society actors can align insights and explore collective strategies.

Conference Rationale

As China’s economic and diplomatic presence grows in South Asia, the need for coordinated regional analysis becomes more urgent. BODHI SAMBAD seeks to bridge knowledge gaps by spotlighting:

  • The interplay between infrastructure investment and sovereignty
  • Economic interdependence and trade policy alignment
  • Security implications for maritime and land boundaries
  • Regional platforms for collective negotiation

Goals for 2026

Our objectives are to foster collaboration, distil actionable policy recommendations, and strengthen networks across South Asia. The 3-day programme includes keynote addresses, panels, workshops, and breakout sessions designed for deep engagement.

This conference is an entry point for sustained dialogue that supports informed decision-making at national and regional levels.
